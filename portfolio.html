ND WBD Interactive

I am the North Dakota's Watershed Boundary Dataset (WBD) Data Steward. As primary Data Steward, I strive to make the data accessible to everyone. This ArcGIS Online application was built for NDDEQ staff as well as our local watershed coordinators to use when they hold outreach and educational events.

Arsenic in Groundwater

There was a need to display arsenic concentrations in groundwater for North Dakota, but I was restricted in my ability to display individual sample results due to privacy concerns. The result is this web application summarizing 22 years of groundwater monitoring data by aquifer.

"How Slender is North Dakota's Regulatory Environment?" Infographic

In an assignment for Geography 572 at University of Wisconsin-Madison, I explored the relationship between the amount of barrels of oil produced to the number of regulatory staff in the top 10 oil-producing states in the United States.

Jerusalem and Tolna Outlets in the Devils Lake Basin, North Dakota

My first foray into fieldwork in North Dakota was a study of the pre-European settlement outlet of the Devils Lake Basin. Only in North Dakota can a glacial geologist get a backhoe to dig their cross sections! The results were published as North Dakota Geological Survey Report of Investigation 100, or RI 100

The Grand Tour Bicycle Race

Another course assignment for Geography 572 was to create a map for a proposed multi-stage international bicycle race. I thought it was interesting that there was a Grand Forks North Dakota as well as a Grand Forks, British Columbia, so I proposed a 21-day race route beginning in the Great Plains and running over the Canadian Rockies. I styled it after the Grand Tour maps of Europe from the early 1900's
